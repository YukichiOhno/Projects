Matcha Milk Tea

Matcha milk tea is a delightful beverage that we can't get enough of - it's sweet, rich, and earthy! Not to mention the tiny yet moreish boba pearls.

Ingredients

  • 1 Teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 2 Tablespoons hot water
  • 2 Tablespoonshoney
  • 240ml Almond or oat milk
  • 50g Boba (pearls)

Instructions

  1. Combine matcha powder with hot water in a small bowl and let bloom for 1 to 2 minutes. Set aside
  2. Combine the honey, matcha tea, almond milk, and ice into your Vitamix container in the order listed and secure the lid.
  3. Select Variable 1 and start the machine. Quickly speed up to Variable 10 and blend for 30 seconds.
  4. Pour over cooked tapioca pearls in a tall glass and enjoy.

Angel Food

Strawberry banana smoothie is made made of strawberries, bananas, non-fat milk, and turbinado sugar, all blended together. It's a wonderfully lush and creamy banana strawberry drink!

Ingredients

  • 100g Frozen Strawberries
  • 1 Banana
  • 100ml Sugar
  • 10ml Vanilla Extract
  • Non-fat Milk
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until Smooth.
  3. Serve and enjoy

Blue Hawaiian

It’s bright and blue, making it a fun tropical drink. The combination of coconut and pineapple flavors with a hint of orange is refreshingly sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Light Rum
  • 1 oz Blue Curacao
  • 3 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 3/4 oz Cream of Coconut
  • Pineapple slice for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine Light Rum, Blue Curacao, Pineapple Juice and Cream of Coconut in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add about a 1/2 cup of ice and shake until the outside of your shaker is cold.
  3. Fill your hurricane glass with ice.
  4. Pour your mixture into your glass.
  5. Decorate with a slice of pineapple, Maraschino cherry and a tropical drink umbrella.
