Avocado on French Toast

Avocado on French toast is a delicious and unique twist on the classic breakfast dish. The creamy, rich texture of mashed avocado pairs perfectly with the slightly sweet and eggy flavor of French toast, creating a decadent and filling meal. To make avocado on French toast, ripe avocado is mashed and spread onto a slice of bread before being dipped in a mixture of eggs, milk, and seasonings. The bread is then fried in a pan until golden brown and crispy, creating the perfect base for the creamy avocado topping. The finished dish is often served with additional toppings such as fresh avocado slices, chopped herbs, or a drizzle of maple syrup for added sweetness. Avocado on French toast is a satisfying and nutrient-dense breakfast option that is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of bread (thickly sliced bread like brioche or challah is recommended)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter or oil
  • Fresh avocado slices
  • Chopped herbs, such as cilantro or parsley
  • Maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pit. Scoop out the flesh and mash it with a fork in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Heat a large non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add the butter or oil to the pan.
  4. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure both sides are coated evenly.
  5. Place the bread slices in the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Once the French toast is cooked, spread a generous amount of mashed avocado onto each slice.
  7. Top with fresh avocado slices, chopped herbs, and a drizzle of maple syrup if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious avocado on French toast!

Quinoa Fruit Salad

A quinoa fruit salad is a colorful and healthy dish that combines cooked quinoa with a variety of fresh fruits, creating a refreshing and nutritious meal. Quinoa is a high-protein grain that is also gluten-free, making it an excellent choice for salads and other healthy dishes. The fruit used in the salad can vary depending on personal preference and seasonal availability, but common choices include berries, melons, kiwi, grapes, and citrus fruits. To make the salad, the cooked quinoa is tossed with the chopped fruit, creating a colorful and delicious blend of textures and flavors. The salad is then dressed with a mixture of lemon or lime juice, honey, and a touch of olive oil, which enhances the natural sweetness of the fruits and complements the nutty flavor of the quinoa. Quinoa fruit salad is a great option for a light lunch or as a healthy side dish for any meal, providing a satisfying and nutritious combination of carbohydrates, fiber, protein, and vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup diced strawberries
  • 1 cup diced mango
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup diced kiwi
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa in a fine mesh strainer under cold running water and drain.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water and quinoa to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot and simmer until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and let the quinoa cool to room temperature. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa with the diced strawberries, mango, blueberries, and kiwi.
  5. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, honey, and olive oil to make the dressing.
  6. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and stir until everything is well coated. Add the chopped mint and stir to combine.
  7. Chill the quinoa fruit salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Before serving, give the salad a gentle toss to redistribute the dressing and garnish with additional mint leaves if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious and healthy quinoa fruit salad!

Tofu Scramble

A tofu scramble is a vegan alternative to scrambled eggs that is made by crumbling firm tofu and cooking it with vegetables and spices. The texture and flavor of the dish is similar to scrambled eggs, but tofu is used instead of eggs, making it a popular protein-rich breakfast option for vegans and those who are looking for a plant-based alternative to traditional breakfast foods. The tofu is typically seasoned with turmeric, which gives it a yellow color and a slightly spicy flavor, and mixed with vegetables such as onions, peppers, and spinach for added nutrition and flavor. Overall, a tofu scramble is a healthy, delicious, and easy-to-make breakfast dish that can be customized to suit individual taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • Firm tofu
  • Olive oil
  • Onion, diced
  • Bell pepper, diced
  • Spinach or kale, chopped
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced onions and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Add diced bell peppers and continue to saut√© for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Crumble the tofu into the pan using your hands or a fork and mix it with the onions and peppers.
  5. Add turmeric, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the pan and stir everything together.
  6. Saut√© the mixture for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tofu is lightly browned and heated through.
  7. Add chopped spinach or kale to the pan and continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the greens are wilted and tender.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  9. Serve your delicious tofu scramble hot with your favorite breakfast sides, such as toast, avocado, or fresh fruit.

Chickpea Shawarma Wrap

A Chickpea Shawarma Wrap is a popular Middle Eastern street food that has become a favorite in many parts of the world. This dish typically consists of spiced and roasted chickpeas that are combined with a variety of fresh vegetables, such as lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cucumbers, and then wrapped in a warm pita bread. The wrap is often finished with a creamy and tangy sauce, such as tahini or tzatziki, which adds a burst of flavor and richness to the dish. The chickpeas are marinated in a blend of aromatic spices, such as cumin, coriander, and paprika, which gives them a delicious and savory taste. This dish is both healthy and filling, making it a great option for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 small pita breads
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the chickpeas, olive oil, cumin, coriander, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper until well coated.
  3. Spread the chickpeas out on a baking sheet and roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until crispy and browned.
  4. Warm the pita breads in the oven for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Spread each pita bread with a spoonful of hummus.
  6. Top each pita bread with a generous amount of the roasted chickpeas, lettuce, tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, and parsley.
  7. Squeeze a lemon wedge over the top of each wrap, if desired.
  8. Roll up the pita bread tightly to enclose the filling.
  9. Serve and enjoy!

Sesame Soba noodles

Sesame Soba Noodles is a flavorful and nutritious dish that is popular in Japanese cuisine. This dish features a bed of soba noodles, which are made from buckwheat flour and have a slightly nutty flavor. The noodles are typically served cold or at room temperature and are tossed in a savory sesame sauce, which is made from a blend of sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey or sugar. The dish is then topped with a variety of vegetables, such as carrots, cucumbers, and scallions, and garnished with sesame seeds for an extra nutty flavor and texture. This dish is not only delicious, but also healthy and filling, making it a perfect option for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces soba noodles
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced scallions
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook soba noodles according to package instructions, then drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey or sugar, garlic, and red pepper flakes (if using) to make the sesame sauce.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, toss the cooked soba noodles with the sesame sauce until evenly coated.
  4. Add the sliced scallions, shredded carrots, and chopped cucumber to the mixing bowl with the noodles, and toss until everything is well combined.
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the noodle mixture before serving.
  6. Serve cold or at room temperature, and enjoy!

Vegan Burrito Bowl

A Vegan Burrito Bowl is a delicious and healthy meal that is packed with nutrients and flavors. This bowl is typically made up of a base of rice and beans, which are then topped with a variety of fresh vegetables, herbs, and spices. The bowl can be customized with different types of beans, grains, and vegetables depending on personal preference and seasonal availability. Some common additions include avocado, tomato, cilantro, lettuce, corn, and salsa. This dish is also often drizzled with a creamy dressing made from avocado or cashews, which adds richness and creaminess to the bowl. This filling and flavorful dish is a great option for a satisfying and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Optional Toppings

  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Jalapenos
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Vegan sour cream or cashew cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the brown rice according to the package instructions.
  2. While the rice is cooking, heat the black beans in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.
  3. Once the rice and beans are ready, divide the rice evenly between two bowls.
  4. Add the black beans, avocado slices, diced tomato, corn kernels, chopped cilantro, and diced red onion on top of the rice.
  5. Squeeze lime wedges over the top of the bowl, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add any additional toppings of your choice, such as salsa, guacamole, jalapenos, shredded lettuce, or vegan sour cream or cashew cream.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Vegan Burrito Bowl!

Spaghetti & Spinach

Spaghetti and spinach with sun-dried tomatoes is a delicious and nutritious pasta dish that is bursting with flavor. The dish is made by cooking spaghetti until al dente and then tossing it with fresh spinach leaves, sun-dried tomatoes, and a flavorful garlic and olive oil sauce. The sun-dried tomatoes add a sweet and tangy flavor that pairs perfectly with the earthy spinach and nutty spaghetti. This dish is easy to make and can be enjoyed as a light and healthy meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes (packed in oil), drained and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, stirring constantly for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the spinach leaves to the skillet and saut√© for 1-2 minutes or until they start to wilt.
  4. Stir in the chopped sun-dried tomatoes and cook for an additional minute.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the spinach and sun-dried tomatoes. Toss everything together until well combined.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper, to taste.
  7. Serve the spaghetti and spinach with sun-dried tomatoes hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy!

Cabbage Soup

Cabbage soup with veggies is a hearty and comforting soup that is packed with nutritious vegetables. It usually consists of chopped cabbage, carrots, onions, celery, and sometimes other veggies like potatoes, tomatoes, or bell peppers. The vegetables are cooked in a flavorful broth made from vegetable stock or water, and seasoned with herbs and spices such as bay leaves, thyme, garlic, and black pepper. The result is a delicious and healthy soup that is perfect for warming you up on a chilly day, or for when you need a healthy and filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage, chopped
  • 3-4 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 1-2 potatoes, peeled and diced (optional)
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 8 cups of vegetable stock or water
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large soup pot over medium heat and add a bit of oil or cooking spray.
  2. Add the chopped onions, sliced celery, and minced garlic, and saut√© for a few minutes until softened.
  3. Add the sliced carrots, diced potatoes (if using), and chopped cabbage to the pot.
  4. Pour in the vegetable stock or water and add the can of diced tomatoes, including the liquid.
  5. Add the bay leaf, dried thyme, and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer and cover the pot.
  7. Let the soup simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Chickpea and Quinoa Bowl

A quinoa-chickpea bowl is a nutritious and filling dish that combines cooked quinoa with protein-packed chickpeas and a variety of fresh vegetables. Quinoa is a gluten-free grain that is high in protein, fiber, and various nutrients, while chickpeas are a great source of plant-based protein and fiber. The vegetables used in the bowl can vary depending on personal preference and seasonal availability, but common choices include leafy greens, roasted sweet potatoes, cherry tomatoes, and avocado. To make the dish, the cooked quinoa is mixed with the chickpeas and vegetables, along with a flavorful dressing made from lemon juice, olive oil, and herbs. The resulting dish is a delicious and satisfying combination of textures and flavors, making it a perfect option for a healthy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water in a fine-mesh strainer and add to a pot with 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until quinoa is tender and water is absorbed.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together cooked quinoa, chickpeas, bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
  4. Drizzle dressing over the quinoa-chickpea mixture and toss to combine.
  5. Divide the mixture into bowls and top with sliced avocado and crumbled feta cheese (if using).
  6. Serve with lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy!
